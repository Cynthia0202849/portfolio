Mandazi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and cardamom.
  2. Add coconut milk to form dough.
  3. Roll out, cut into triangles, and deep-fry until golden.
