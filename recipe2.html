Kenyan Pilau

Ingredients

For the Rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef or chicken
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 cups water/stock
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Pilau Masala

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp cardamom pods
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp turmeric

Instructions

  1. Toast spices: Dry roast whole spices for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, then grind to powder.
  2. Brown meat: In a pot, heat oil and brown meat with onions, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Build flavor: Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Stir in spice powder and turmeric.
  4. Cook rice: Add rinsed rice and mix well. Pour in hot water/stock, bring to boil, then simmer covered for 18-20 minutes.
  5. Rest & serve: Remove from heat, let stand for 5 minutes, then fluff with fork. Garnish with fried onions.

Pro Tips

• For extra flavor, use beef stock instead of water
• Add potatoes for a heartier version (add with rice)
• Let the pilau rest covered for 5 mins after cooking for perfect texture
